Weapon Specialty Feats

Whenever a Weapon Specialty feature mentions your weapon’s damage dice, it only refers to the dice that you roll for that weapon (as listed on the weapons table and with weapon scaling). For example, the weapon damage dice of a Flametongue Dagger that deals 1d4 piercing + 2d6 fire would refer only to the 1d4 (if the wielder was at least level 5, this would become 2d4 because of weapon scaling).

Weapon Specialty: Axe Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the axe group (battlaxe, greataxe, handaxe). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Graze. If you make an attack roll with a weapon in this group and you miss, you deal damage to the creature equal to the ability modifier you used for the attack roll (minimum 0). This damage is the same type as the weapon's.

  • Level 1: Slasher. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, the creature's movement speed is reduced by 10 ft. until the end of their next turn. A creature can only be affected by this once per turn.

  • Level 3: Vex. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, you have advantage on your next attack roll against that creature until the end of your next turn.

  • Level 5: Rampage. Once per turn, if you score a critical hit or reduce a creature's hit points to 0 with a weapon in this group, you can make one additional attack as part of your attack action.

  • Level 7: Sharpened Blade. One per attack, when you roll damage for an attack that you made with this weapon, you can choose to reroll one of the weapon's damage dice, but must use the new roll.

Weapon Specialty: Bow Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the bow group (longbow and shortbow). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Critical Shot. Whenever you land a critical hit with a weapon in this group, you can double your Dexterity modifier for the damage roll.

  • Level 1: Slowing. Whenever you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, the creature's movement speed is reduced by 10 ft. until the end of their next turn. A creature can only be affected by this once per turn.

  • Level 3: Farsight. Attacking at long range doesn't impose disadvantage on ranged weapon attack rolls that you make with a weapon in this group.

  • Level 5: Precision Attack. Once per turn, when you make an attack with a weapon in this group, you can give yourself advantage on the attack roll.

  • Level 7: Swift Strike. Once per turn, when you land a critical hit with a weapon in this group, you can make one additional attack as part of your attack action.

Weapon Specialty: Club Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the club group (club, flail, greatclub, mace, maul, morningstar, warhammer, and warpick). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Pulverizer. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, the crature's AC is reduced by 1 until the end of their next turn. A creature can only be affected by this once per turn.

  • Level 1: Topple. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, you can force that creature to make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier (minimum 0)). On a failed saving throw, the creature falls Prone.

  • Level 3: Vex. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, you have advantage on your next attack roll against that creature until the end of your next turn.

  • Level 5: Heavy Swing. Once per turn, when you hit a Large-sized or smaller creature with a weapon in this group, you can forcefully move that creature 10 ft. away from yourself.

  • Level 7: Earth Tremor. As an action, you can slam your weapon into the ground and create a shockwave. When you do, you cast the Earth Tremor spell at 2nd level without expending a spell slot or needing any components. The DC for the spell is 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier (minimum 0). You can do this a number of times per long rest equal to your Strength modifier (minimum 1).

Weapon Specialty: Crossbow Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the crossbow group (crossbow, hand crossbow, heavy crossbow). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Close Quarters Combatant. Being within 5 ft. of a hostile creature or your target does not impose disadvantage on any attack roll that you make with a weapon in this group.

  • Level 1: Slowing. Whenever you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, the creature's movement speed is reduced by 10 ft. until the end of their next turn. A creature can only be affected by this once per turn.

  • Level 3: Trick Shot. Before you make an attack roll with a weapon in this group, you can choose to take a -3 penalty to the attack roll. If you do, you can increase the weapon's damage by 5 if you hit.

  • Level 5: Piercer. One per attack, when you roll damage for an attack that you made with this weapon, you can choose to reroll one of the weapon's damage dice, but must use the new roll.

  • Level 7: Vex. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, you have advantage on your next attack roll against that creature until the end of your next turn.

Weapon Specialty: Monk Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the monk group (bo staff, chakram, nunchucks, quarterstaff, sais, shuriken). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Kensei's Shot. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack with a weapon in the Monk group, you can increase the damage by an additional roll of your weapon’s damage die (if attacking with a dagger with a damage die of 1d4, you roll an additional d4. This does not increase with weapon scaling).

  • Level 1: Monastic Parry. While wielding a melee weapon in this group, your AC is increased by 1.

  • Level 3: Disarming Attack. Once per turn, when you make an attack against a Large or smaller creature with a weapon in this group, you can force the creature to make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier(minimum 0)). On a failed saving throw, the creature drops a weapon or item of your choice. You can use this feature a number of times per short or long rest equal to your proficiency bonus.

  • Level 5: Sweeping. Once per turn, when you take the attack action while wielding a melee weapon in this group, you can forgo one of your attacks to make all creatures of your choice within that weapon’s range make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier (minimum 0)). On a failed saving throw, a creature is knocked Prone.

  • Level 7: Swift Strike. Once per turn, when you land a critical hit with a weapon in this group, you can make one additional attack as part of your attack action.

Weapon Specialty: Polearm Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the polearm group (glaive, halberd). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Polearm Fighter. While wielding a weapon in this group, creatures provoke opportunity attacks from you when they enter the melee range of your weapon. Additionally, creatures provoke opportunity attacks even if they take the Disengage action if you’re wielding a weapon in this group.

  • Level 1: Sentinel. Whenever you hit a creature with an opportunity attack with a weapon in this group, that creature's movement speed is reduced to 0 until the end of their turn.

  • Level 3: Protector. While you're wielding a weapon in this group, if a creature within your weapon's range makes an attack roll against a creature other than you, you can use your reaction to make a melee attack against the attacking creature. Your attack goes before the creature’s attack roll and, if you hit, the attack roll of the other creature is made at disadvantage.

  • Level 5: Polearm Master. When you take the attack action and attack with a weapon in this group, you can use a bonus action to make a melee attack with the opposite end of the weapon. This attack uses the same ability modifier as the primary attack and deals the same amount of damage as the primary attack, but deals bludgeoning damage instead of slashing or piercing.

  • Level 7: Lunging Attack. Once per turn, when you make an attack with a weapon in this group, you can increase the range of your weapon by 5 ft.

Weapon Specialty: Rogue Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the rogue group (dagger, scimitar, shuriken). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Assassin's Critical. Whenever you land a critical hit with a weapon in this group, you can triple your weapon's damage dice instead of doubling it.

  • Level 1: Slasher. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, the creature's movement speed is reduced by 10 ft. until the end of their next turn. A creature can only be affected by this once per turn.

  • Level 3: Vex. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, you have advantage on your next attack roll against that creature until the end of your next turn.

  • Level 5: Cunning Tactics. While wielding a weapon in this group, you can take the disengage action as a bonus action. When you do, your movement speed increases by 10 ft. until the end of your turn.

  • Level 7: Evasive Fighting. While wielding a weapon in this group, you gain +1 to your AC.

Weapon Specialty: Scythe Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the scythe group (kusarigama, scythe, sickle). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Graze. If you make an attack roll with a weapon in this group and you miss, you deal damage to the creature equal to the ability modifier you used for the attack roll (minimum 0). This damage is the same type as the weapon's.

  • Level 1: Vex. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, you have advantage on your next attack roll against that creature until the end of your next turn.

  • Level 3: Gashing Wounds. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, the crature's AC is reduced by 1 until the end of their next turn. A creature can only be affected by this once per turn.

  • Level 5: Sweeping. Once per turn, when you take the attack action while wielding a melee weapon in this group, you can forgo one of your attacks to make all creatures of your choice within that weapon’s range make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier (minimum 0)). On a failed saving throw, a creature is knocked Prone.

  • Level 7: Reaper. Once per turn, when making a melee attack with a weapon in this group, you can attempt to hit a second creature with the same attack, provided that the second creature is in range of your weapon and the creature would be hit by the initial attack roll. For example, if there are two enemies in your weapon's range with 12 AC and you rolled a 16 on your initial attack roll, both creatures are hit by the attack.

Weapon Specialty: Spear Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the spear group (javelin, lance, longspear, spear, trident). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Sentinel. Whenever you hit a creature with an opportunity attack with a weapon in this group, that creature's movement speed is reduced to 0 until the end of their turn.

  • Level 1: Warding Attack. Whenever you hit a creature with a melee attack with a weapon in this group, that creature cannot willingly move closer towards you until the end of their next turn.

  • Level 3: Lunging Attack. Once per turn, when you make an attack with a weapon in this group, you can increase the range of your weapon by 5 ft.

  • Level 5: Precision Attack. Once per turn, when you make an attack with a weapon in this group, you can give yourself advantage on the attack roll.

  • Level 7: Goring Strike. Whenever you land a critical hit with a weapon in this group, the weapon's damage dice are tripled instead of doubled.

Weapon Specialty: Sword Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the sword group (greatsword, longsword, rapier, scimitar, shortsword). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Critical Blade. Whenever you land a critical hit with a weapon in this group, you can double your Strength or Dexterity modifier for the damage roll.

  • Level 1: Parrying Blade. While you're wielding a weapon in this group, your AC is increased by 1.

  • Level 3: Graze. If you make an attack roll with a weapon in this group and you miss, you deal damage to the creature equal to the ability modifier you used for the attack roll (minimum 0). This damage is the same type as the weapon's.

  • Level 5: Vex. When you hit a creature with a weapon in this group, you have advantage on your next attack roll against that creature until the end of your next turn.

  • Level 7: Flourishing Blade. Once per turn, when you miss an attack roll with a weapon in this group as part of your attack action, you can make one additional attack as part of the same action.

Weapon Specialty: Whip Weapons

1st Level Feat

You are well-trained with weapons in the whip group (bullwhip, whip). You gain the following benefits based on your level:

  • Level 1: Graze. If you make an attack roll with a weapon in this group and you miss, you deal damage to the creature equal to the ability modifier you used for the attack roll (minimum 0). This damage is the same type as the weapon's.

  • Level 1: Whip Expert. Whenever you make an attack with a weapon in this group, you can increase the weapon's range by 5 ft.

  • Level 3: Tripping Attack. Once per turn, when you hit a Large-sized or smaller creature with a weapon in this group, you can force that creature to make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier (minimum 0)). On a failed save, the creature falls Prone.

  • Level 5: Prehensile Whip. If you take the attack action while wielding a weapon in this group, you can forgo one of your attacks to have your weapon retrieve one item within its range. The weight of item cannot exceed 10 x your Strength or Dexterity modififer (minimum 0) in lbs. You can choose to have the item retrieved appear on the ground at your feet onr in a free hand. If a creature is holding the item that you wish to grab, that creature must make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier (minimum 0)). On a failed saving throw, the creature is forced to let go of the item and your whip grabs it.

  • Level 7: Entangling Whip. If you take the attack acttion while wielding a weapon in this group, you can forgo one of your attacks to attempt to entangle someone in your weapon. Choose one creature in your weapon's range and have them make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier (minimum 0)). On a failed saving throw, the creature is Grappled. If the creature fails the saving throw by 5 or more, that creature is Restrained while they are Grappled. Once a creature is Grappled this way, you cannot make any attacks with the weapon that they are Grappled with. A creature can use their action to repeat the saving throw in order to break free.
